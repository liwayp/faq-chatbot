Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What are your business hours?
Our customer service is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. Weekend support is available via email only.

2. How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link within 5 minutes.

3. What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

4. How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team via:
- Email: support@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-123-4567
- Live chat on our website during business hours

5. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

6. Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

7. Is my data secure?
We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect your data. Our servers are hosted in SOC 2 compliant data centers with 99.9% uptime guarantee.

8. Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all our plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

9. How many users can I add to my account?
The number of users depends on your plan:
- Basic: Up to 5 users
- Professional: Up to 25 users
- Enterprise: Unlimited users

10. Do you offer training for new users?
Yes, we provide free onboarding sessions and have an extensive knowledge base with video tutorials. Enterprise customers get dedicated training sessions.
